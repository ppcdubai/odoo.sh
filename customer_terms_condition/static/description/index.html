Customer Terms And Conditions

Features

Manage customer terms and condition added in (Terms And Conditions). those terms and condition will be displayed in note field of sale order and comment field on account invoice.

So we can get individual terms and condition to be printed on Sale Quotation, Sale Order and Sale Invoice Either Customer Specific or Company Specific (Global - incase if customer doesn't have terms and condition)
